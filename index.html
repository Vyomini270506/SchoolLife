Grade 1 to Grade 5

Primary school days are filled with colorful classrooms, playful learning activities, and the joy of making new friends, where the basics of knowledge are laid with a mix of fun and laughter, creating cherished memories of childhood.These 5 years are something which a person misses a lot in their later life. At this stage our dreams are very common and they change frequently.


Some important things at this stage

  1. Finish Homework fast so that you can go outside and play
  2. Carefree days and that too without academic pressure :)
  3. People mostly ask your age not your marks
  4. There are exams for Drawing,music and you study hard for these exams!!
  5. Study two days before exams and score A+ ;)
